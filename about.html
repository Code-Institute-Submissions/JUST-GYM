who we are

We at Just Gym are a team of professionals who look after each other like family. Each member of our team is fully qualified to at least a level 3 industry standard. The thing we're most passionate about is keeping fit but also having fun whilst doing it. What's life without a little enjoyment? We're here to guide you through your journey no matter what goals you wish to achieve, nothing is impossible at Just Gym! Read on below for more information or Join Now to embark on your new journey.

what we do

Guide & Nurture

We guide individuals into a new and exciting life. We show people how to live life to the max. Whether someone is brand new to the gym scene or whether they're regular gym-goers, there is always something new to learn. There's always something different to try! Our experienced team of Personal Trainers are here to guide and nurture you through the exciting steps of your journey. This can be done by tailored personal sessions or bespoke exercise programs. There is always someone on site to guide you, no matter the time or day!

Teach & Motivate

We offer a wide breadth of classes to keep your interest peaked and to offer something a little different to your usual day-to-day business. Our gyms offer inclusive classes for everyone, so whether you’re after a fast-paced morning cycle, a challenging lunch-time circuit or a relaxing evening yoga class, you’ll find plenty to keep you busy throughout the week.

Help Connect

We like to think of Just Gym as a kind of social hub. We're proud to say that our team and our members all look out for each other in ways that we never imagined. We're absolutely positive that within a few short periods you'll make new friends and embark on exciting journeys with people you never would have met if it wasn't for your phenomenal decision to join Just Gym! We hold weekly get-togethers such as 5k runs and coffee mornings. We invite you to get stuck in!

Transform Life

We understand that one of the most difficult parts of maintaining a fit physique or shedding a few pounds is the diet aspect. Rest assured and fear not because we have our very own fully qualified in-house dietician who'll be able to point you in the right direction. Whether you're losing weight or gaining muscle, our in-house team will be able to provide you with all the information you need to get started. Don't worry for a second because all of this and beyond it available to you as part of your monthly subscription!
